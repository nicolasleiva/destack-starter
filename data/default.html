Satellite MRV 

We utilize our proprietary models for change detection and land use, thereby promoting good agricultural practices and monitoring agricultural deforestation.

 Cover crop

Regenerative agriculture relies on cover crops to enhance soil, reduce erosion, and store carbon naturally. Satellites are used to detect these cover crops by analyzing vegetation indices during non-growing seasons, allowing stakeholders to monitor their prevalence across extensive areas


 


 Tillage detection

 Reducing soil disturbance and leaving crop residue on the soil surface is crucial in regenerative agriculture, enhancing soil structure and carbon emission reduction. We classify tillage practices (no-till, min-till, conventional) by analyzing reflectance percentages in specific wavelength bands, mainly in the near-infrared and shortwaveinfrared spectra.


 Crop Classification

  Using our crop type classification models, we can analyze geospatial and temporal crop data to identify crop rotations and diversity. This enables large-scale monitoring of these practices, valuable for sustainable agriculture verification and supply chain monitoring.



 

 Forestation/

Deforestation

We perform precise measurements of tree plantations and evaluate any deforestation that may have occurred over the past 8 years. This comprehensive and detailed examination enables us to assess your level of regenerative agriculture effectively, giving us a fuller understanding of any instances of forest clearing. .


Environmental Traceability on Blockchain

 Our blockchain-based environmental traceability feature ensures real-time tracking of agricultural products. Leveraging this technology, every step of the production process is transparently and immutably recorded on the blockchain, from planting to distribution. This enables consumers and stakeholders to scan the QR code and verify the origin of products, ensuring transparency in the supply chain and enabling assessment of production systems.
